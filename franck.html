Profil: Nijimbere Franck

Nijimbere Franck or Einstein as called in the club as well as in the classroom Co-founded the club with his little brotherNiyongere Florentin and his neighborNduwayo Arsene . His passion for computers began when he first touched a computer. He was then 9 years old and since then he has always wanted to learn more in this field.He was the first President of the club. Aware of the problems faced by his company in computer science, he decided to create the BMP Computer Club to help with their training in this field. He has contributed a lot to all the departments with mentoring as well as the creation of the game Plusoumoins. He is a feared programmer. He is now pursuing his studies in the United States of America. He is motivated to improve the development in his home country.
