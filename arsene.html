Profil: Nduwayo Arsene

Nduwayo Arsene is a 22 years old man who participated in the creation of the club in 2012. He played a role of mentor for both Nijimbere Franck and Niyongere Florentin . His high experience in computer science and skills gave him the presidency of the club . his interest is oriented to programming, web designing, and economic skills in our bank department. Arsene is a strategic young man who has demonstrated his economic capacities nationally in 2015 and came out the best in the whole country. He is considerate as a founder member and Big brother for all the members. He went in Uganda to pursues his studies. Without Arsene, the club wouldn’t grow like it did. He innovates, creates and gives services like no other member of the club. His talent goes beyond the keyboard, his hands are dedicated to the piano as well.
