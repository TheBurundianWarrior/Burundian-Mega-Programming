Profil: Niyongere Florentin

Niyongere Florentin or Flo as called in the club is an 18 years old young man
He stands out from the crowd with my creativity since a young age surmounted by a high interest in computer technology. He Co-founded this Computer Science club with his older BrotherNijimbere Franck when he was 13 years old.
At that time, the Brothers got the idea by observing the current Problems in their society. They associated with a neighborNduwayo Arsène to form a club at the services of citizen and to help the youth not to diverge in the delinquency. When they started the Club, Flo was the youngest member of the club. But he acted as a leader in the Designing Department as well as a big contribution to the Bank project in the Club. His interest is oriented to Programming, Web Designing, 3D Designing, and Networking. He is a Fresh Graduated from High school at King David Academy in Rwanda. After his older Brother and the former BMP president went to pursue his studies, he took the place of President of the club.

The arrival of a saving account in the computer club BMP will bring a lot of dynanism, which will be to our advantage. The Project idea came to mind in 2012 when the club members were in a their usual conference that takes place every weekend, suddently a pipe loosened. The leader of the club used the money that wasn't supposed to be spent to repair the pipe. This was an umpredictable problem that had to be solved absolutly. a year later,Nduwayo Arsene got the idea of saving for such unpredictable problems. We started contributing,Nzosaba Davy got the idea of borrowing money and return it with some interest. This service was only reserved to te club members.Kadogo Rodrigue made used of his economic skills to unable our neiborhood to access the BMP bank services through a system of form filling and exeptionaly mortgage when the amount exeeds limit fixed by our economic team.The community is pleased of our tremedous services both in terms of computer and financial support. Our Database team will make sure that there is no malicious on the informations given. The Bank operates in our unic community in reason of the reliability.We plan to expand it services in a near future.
